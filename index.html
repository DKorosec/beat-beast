BEAT BEAST

  • Imune to damage activates when you spawn and when you take damage. It lasts for few seconds.
  • Shields work as extra HPs. When your HP drops to 0, it will consume 1 shield, to fill back up.
  • Your hp will drop to 0 when hit by a projectile or gradually decrease, when too far away or too close to the beat beast.
    • The pitch / tempo of the music will guide you to the near perfect distance and also the background color (green =good, red = bad).
    • You will regain HP when in the near perfect distance from the beat beast.
    • WHITE projectiles will hurt you. (they gradually turn to dark and are harder to spot - watchout!)
    • GREEN projectiles will heal you to full HP + 1 shield.

  • What are you waiting for? Select a song, start the game and use your mouse to control your character. Beat beast is waiting for you ;)
